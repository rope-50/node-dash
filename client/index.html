What you are watching

This is a from-scratch DASH player: no video library, just the browser's Media Source Extensions. Here is what happens as it plays:

  1. It reads a DASH manifest that lists the available video qualities.
  2. It downloads the video and audio in small chunks, the same way YouTube does.
  3. Partway through it switches to a higher quality on the fly, so the picture visibly gets sharper. Watch the badge (top-right).
  4. It also pauses briefly to simulate buffering, with a loading bar across the top.
